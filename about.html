Our Story in Global Trade

Founded in 2025, OceanArc Exim connects businesses worldwide, providing expert solutions in agriculture exports including rice, pulses, and clothing exports such as sarees and kurtis. Our mission is to enable seamless international trade with transparency and efficiency.

We specialize in end-to-end trade solutions, ensuring compliance, documentation, logistics, and post-delivery support for our partners across Asia, Europe, the Americas, and Africa.
